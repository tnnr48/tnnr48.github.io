Regression Analysis and Statistics

My name is James and I have a bachelor's degree in math and am currently pursuing my master's.

This site is intended as a useful resource for anyone who wants to learn about linear regression analysis and several useful statistics concepts and tools. Through a lot of time spent trying to teach myself this material I got frustrated with a lack of resources or clarity in explanation when they existed. Therefore, the content on this site is, in general, introduced and explained in a thorough way so it can be learned as easily as possible. Note, you will need to have a working understanding of linear algebra and multivariable calculus and there are many good resources for both.
